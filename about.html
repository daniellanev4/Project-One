Meaning Behind My Name

My full name is Daniella Kimberly Nevins. Daniella means God is My Judge. Kimberly means "From the wood of the royal forest" Nevins is an Irish last name, comes from the variant Nevin Meaning: holy, sacred

Why Daniella?

my mother was an illegal Colombian immigrant when she met my father. Their are two reasons why she named me Daniella; Number 1: she has an Uncle named Daniel who was her idle and who she adored Number 2: my grandmother said the name Daniella one day and my mother fell in love with the way my "nanny" said it. My mom said she wanted me to have an American name and a bit unique which is why she added two L's instead of one.
